Frequently Asked Questions

Ibibazo bikunze kubazwa

How do I pay? (Nishyura gute?) +

We accept MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, Visa/Mastercard and cash on delivery across Rwanda.

Do you deliver across Rwanda? +

Yes! We deliver to all 5 provinces of Rwanda. Kigali delivery takes 1–2 days, other provinces 2–4 days.

What is your return policy? +

We offer a 7-day return policy on all unused items in original packaging. Contact us on WhatsApp for quick help.

How do I track my order? +

Once shipped, you receive an SMS with a tracking code. You can also check your order status in your member account.

Is my payment secure? +

Absolutely. All payments are processed securely. MTN MoMo transactions are fully encrypted and safe.

Can I change or cancel my order? +

Yes! You can change or cancel your order within 2 hours of placing it. Contact us immediately on WhatsApp.
