Who We Are

Elissa Online Store was born from a simple dream — to make quality products accessible to every Rwandan. Founded with the spirit of Agaciro (dignity and self-worth), we believe every customer deserves the best.

From the hills of Kigali to every province of Rwanda, we deliver electronics, fashion, beauty and more — with care, speed and a smile.

Our name "Elissa" means God is my promise, and we make a promise to you — quality you can trust, every single time.

Our Story

How Elissa Online Store came to life

The Beginning
